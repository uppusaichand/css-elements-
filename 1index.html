Impressive performers in IPL 2021, Ruturaj Gaikwad, Venkatesh Iyer, Harshal Patel, Ruturaj Gaikwad and Avesh Khan have been named in a refreshed 16-man T20I squad that will take on New Zealand on at home. The Chetan Sharmal-led senior selection committee also named Rohit Sharma as the captain of the squad, taking over from Virat Kohli, while KL Rahul will be the designated vice-captain.
